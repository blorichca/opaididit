What are AI Chatbots?
AI chatbots are kinda like these Siri/Alexa (smart assitants), but actually smart. When you ask to ‘Play Taylor Swift,’ they won’t reply with ‘Calling Mom.’ They really get you.
These are the main 4 AI elements of them.
  • They understand you best: They use advanced language processing to understand your intent and natural way of speaking.
  • Information Powerhouses: AI chatbots are trained on massive datasets, giving them access to a vast range of knowledge.
  • Helpful for Everyone: Their responses adapt to your knowledge level, whether you're a beginner or an expert.
  • Constantly Learning: Each interaction helps them improve, making them progressively smarter.
What can they do?
  • Got Your Legal Back: Analyze contracts and legal documents, simplifying complex language.
  • Physical & Mental Trainer: Provide workout guidance, exercise advice, and mental health support.
  • Financial Advisor: Help you discover financial tools, compare your options, and make sound money choices.
